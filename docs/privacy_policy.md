# Privacy Policy for BleRemoteControl

**Effective date:** 2024-01-01

This privacy policy describes how our BleRemoteControl application collects, uses, and shares information.

## Information We Collect

Our application uses the device's camera for the sole purpose of scanning QR codes to provision the Bluetooth device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, images, or videos from your camera.

## How We Use Information

The camera is used only for the QR code scanning functionality within the app. The data from the QR code is processed locally on your device and is not sent to our servers or any third-party services.

## Information Sharing

We do not share any information with third parties.
